Curriculum-Based GeoGebra Resource Matrix

for Grade 9 Geometry

This matrix presents selected GeoGebra resources aligned with the Grade 9 Geometry curriculum. It maps each interactive material to its corresponding content domain,

learning competencies, content standards, and performance standards. The purpose of this matrix is to provide a structured guide for integrating GeoGebra into

classroom instruction while ensuring alignment with curriculum requirements and learning outcomes.
